Frequently Asked Questions

Does TipiAI support different time zones?

Yes of course! TipiAI looks up the timezone setting in your Slack profile and makes sure that 10am is not 3am where you live.

You may want to double-check that the timezone settings are up-to-date, because Slack doesn't do this automatically for you.

You can also ask the bot to change you preferred time for question to be asked.

How many times does TipiAI ask a question?

TipiAI asks questions 3 times a week, on Monday, Wednesday and Friday. Then on Tuesday, Thursday and Saturday posts replies from all the members of your team.

You can ask the bot to change frequency depending on your needs.

What if i get stuck?

You can always email us at alex(at)tipiai.com or ask bot for help. We will be happy to assist you with your inquiry.

What is Tipi?

Tipi is old cone-shape tent, used by native Americans for ceremonial needs. They celebrated and had fun there :)

Can I change the Questions?

Absolutely! We will be happy to see the questions you want TipiAiI to ask your team. Juse email us your request and we will add you question.

More than that, our engine analyses the responses of the team and find better questions to be asked.

How can i make most of TipiAI?

We strongly advice participating in every question and make it a part of your internal culture.

Read through the replies and get better understanding of your team. You can find great insights on how to approach your team and find connections.

Why do I need TipiAI?

We came to idea of TipiAI from Google's Project Aristotle. They have analysed hundreds of teams and came to the conclusion that teams perform better if they have equal rights to participate in communication. That they would not be judged by speaking up.

We want to help teams build wormer and closer relations inside and care about each other. It would take much longer doing it naturally, you would need tons of beer to replicate this.:)
